Key Aspects

2. Personalization: The model can use remembered information to tailor responses more closely to your interests or needs.

3. Context Management: It enables the AI to keep track of the evolving context and details that might be relevant in future interactions.

Limitations

3. Accuracy Over Time: Information that was relevant in one context may become outdated or less relevant in another. The AI needs to manage the relevance of stored data over time to avoid inaccuracies.

5.Reset Capability: Users generally have the ability to reset or clear the memory, which can help address issues of outdated or unwanted information but also means losing the continuity built up over time.

These limitations reflect a balance between creating a helpful, personalized interaction and maintaining user privacy and data integrity.
